About AIOS

Welcome to the All In One Students Portal, a comprehensive platform specially curated for Amrita University students. This portal serves as your one-stop destination for academic resources, study tools, and campus information.

Created by students, for students, this portal aims to enhance your educational journey at Amrita by providing easy access to valuable resources that support your academic pursuits and campus life.

Our mission is to empower Amrita students with the digital tools and resources they need to excel in their studies, stay informed about campus activities, and connect with the university community.
